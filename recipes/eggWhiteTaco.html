Cooking Instructions:

Recipe is super simple and easy to follow: Add one tsp of Olive Oil to a pan. Start the heat on Med-High and as soon the oil loosens up, spread the oil around the surface. Add Eggwhites to the pan and keep on medium heat until it starts solidifying. In the meantime, start preparing your fresh veggies.

    Instructions:
  1. Dice one tomato and mix 1/4 Cup of Spinach into a bowl.
  2. Place both slices of cheese on 1/2 of the eggwhite. Next, let the become sticky.
  3. Add spinach and tomato ontop of the cheesy substrate. Change heat ---> medium-low
  4. Flip half of the eggwhite to create a "taco". Let it continue to cook on medium low heat for another 3-5 minutes.
  5. Lift eggwhite taco and slide in the tortilla.
  6. Let that cook for another 2-3 minutes. Split in half and enjoy!
